IV. Language Revision
Present Simple (Be + Other Verbs for Habits)
Positive: I eat breakfast. They study English.
Negative: I do not (don't) eat meat. He does not (doesn't) like onions.
Question: Do you speak English? Does she live here?
Third-person -s/-es: loves, goes, does, has, etc.
LANGUAGE FUNCTIONS
Describing daily routines and preferences, e.g.
"Do you go to work at 8 a.m.? Yes, I do, but Anna doesn't start till 10:00 a.m."
"We eat lunch at 2:00 p.m."
