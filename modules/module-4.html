Module Focus:
Handling Reservations, Bookings and Transactions

Aims:

  • Develop ability to initiate and complete bookings and payments across contexts
  • Improve accuracy in recording guest details (dates, names, preferences, numbers)
  • Enhance polite, customer-friendly transaction communication
  • Handle changes, cancellations, and payment queries professionally
  • Build procedures for effective post booking follow-up and updates

Language Skills Focus:

Listening: extract booking requirements and payment details from dialogues

Speaking: confirm reservations, explain options, and handle changes politely

Reading: interpret booking forms, payment terms, and policy notes

Writing: draft confirmations, receipts, and follow-up messages


Language Revision: Key booking verbs and phrases (reserve, confirm, cancel, modify, pay)

Common payment terms and policy language

Polite transactional language and apologies for issues

Past Simple, regular & irregular verbs, Past time expressions


Language Functions: Booking and Payment: requesting and confirming dates, rates, and payment method

Modification: handling changes, upgrades, downgrade, and cancellations

Confirmation: summarizing details and obtaining guest agreement

Clarification: verifying ambiguous details or missing data

Documentation: logging transactions, updates, and next steps

Expressing likes/dislikes, abilities, and future intentions

Describing objects and everyday activities

IV. Language Revision
Past Simple (Introduction)
Regular verbs: add -ed (walked, watched)
Irregular verbs (basic common set): went, had, saw, came
Use: completed past actions at a specific time (yesterday, last week, last year, in 2010, ago)
LANGUAGE FUNCTIONS
Booking and Payment: "Your booking is confirmed for July 25th at 10:00AM."
Modification: "Your flight 7377 has been cancelled. Please contact your travel agent."
Confirmation: "We confirm your request for easy accessibility and full vegan menus."
Clarification: "I want to make sure I understand: is the refund valid until March 30th?"
Expressing likes/dislikes, abilities, and future intentions: "I would like a quiet room with a sea view, please."
Describing objects and everyday activities: "Galaxy Hotel is central, in a tree-lined street close to the metro station and the city opera."

Women play important roles in family life, often managing households and caring for relatives with warmth and resilience. Though customs vary, many Cretan women support equality, education, and cultural preservation, keeping traditions alive while embracing modern opportunities.
