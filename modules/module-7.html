Second Hour

Module Focus:
Language for Safety, Accessibility, and Basic IT Skills

Aims:

  • Explain safety procedures clearly to guests and staff
  • Introduce and practise use of basic IT tools in a hospitality context

Language Skills Focus:

Listening: safety briefings, accessibility requests, IT support prompts

Speaking: clear safety explanations, accessibility accommodations, tech-task explanations

Reading: safety signs and procedures, accessibility guides, basic IT instructions

Writing: concise availability messages, safety instructions and IT-support notes


Language Revision:

Simple Passive:

  • Emphasise the action, not who/what.
  • The action is more important than the actor.

Language Functions: Safety instructions, warnings,

Accessibility communication: options, assistance

IT prompts: basic how-to phrases, making polite requests while giving instructions
