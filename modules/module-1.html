Polite greetings, self-introduction, staff introductions, hotel check-in/out, basic service inquiries.

IV. Language Revision
Subject-Verb-Object (SVO) Simple Sentences
Examples:
- I am a waitress. / You're a tour guide. / He is a housekeeper/cleaner.
- She works in a restaurant.
They like small boutique hotels.
The Verb "to be" (Present)
Forms: I am, you are, he/she/it is, we are, you are, they are.
Contractions: I'm, you're, he's, she's, it's, we're, you're, they're.
LANGUAGE FUNCTIONS
- Location (with prepositions of place: in, on, at), e.g., Nick is in the kitchen. Magda met John at the cruise.
- Introducing oneself and others, e.g., "I'm Paul. This is my colleague, Anna." "Glad to meet you."
- Giving basic personal information, e.g., "We work at the Minos hotel."
