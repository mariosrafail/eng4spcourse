Module Focus:
Greetings, Introductions, and Customer Service Basics

Aims:

Polite greetings, self-introduction, staff introductions, hotel check-in/out, basic service inquiries.


Language Skills Focus:

Listening: short dialogues set in a hotel reception; identify key information, MC questions.

Speaking: polite greetings, self-intro (name, profession, etc.), ask/answer simple questions.

Reading: two short texts about sights or services in Crete with MC or True/False questions.

Writing: guided sentence completion using travel terms and articles, short memo to colleague.


Language Revision: Subject-Verb-Object (SVO) simple sentences, the verb "to be" (present).


Language Functions: greeting and introducing staff and services, offering help and asking clarifying questions.
