Module Focus:
Cultural Awareness, Etiquette, and Team Communication

Aims:

  • Communicate respectfully with diverse guest profiles
  • Improve inter-department communication
  • Use clear, courteous language for cross-cultural communication

Language Skills Focus:

Listening: cultural cues, guest needs, (hotel policies)

Speaking: polite greetings, empathy, colleague/staff communication

Reading: etiquette guidelines, guest profiles, policies

Writing: concise guest messages and notes


Language Revision:

I see...

I understand...

We can...


Language Functions: Identifying problems and stating policies, empathising and resolving problems, communicating cross-culturally (awareness and professional behaviour)
